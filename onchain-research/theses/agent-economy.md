# Agent Economy Thesis

We believe **autonomous AI agents transacting on-chain** is the next
distribution surface for stablecoins. Three legs:

1. **Agents already buy compute and data via API.** Today they pay
   with stored API keys; that breaks the moment one agent needs to pay
   another agent it didn't pre-register with.
2. **HTTP-native crypto rails (x402, AP2)** solve this by letting any
   server respond `402 Payment Required` and any client settle in
   USDC. No accounts, no integrations.
3. **Volume here is fee-elastic in our favor.** Per-call payments
   dwarf retail trading volume on a 24-month view. We want to own
   the chains and stablecoins that capture this float.

## Our sleeve (3% of digital book)

- USDC float (Base) — proxy for stablecoin TVL growth
- Coinbase equity (off-book) — distribution layer for x402
- Small early-stage allocations to teams shipping x402 / A2A wrappers

## What kills this thesis

A regulator forces stablecoin issuers to KYC every counterparty,
including agents. Probability we assign: **15% over 12 months.**
Mitigation: keep sleeve sizing modest.
