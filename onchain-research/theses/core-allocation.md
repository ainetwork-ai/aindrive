# Core Allocation Thesis — Q2 2026

_Last reviewed 2026-04-22 by the IC. Re-priced quarterly._

## Top conviction: Ethereum

**Highest-conviction chain in the book right now is Ethereum.** It is
where institutional flow lands first, where the deepest stablecoin
liquidity sits, and where serious DeFi composability still happens.
The "ETH is dead" trade has been wrong every quarter for three years
running, and we don't intend to be the next desk to repeat it.

Position: ETH spot **38%** of the digital book, with covered calls
written ~10 delta on the top quartile.

## Why not Base or Solana for the top slot

- **Base** is the fastest-growing L2 by user count, but the entire
  ecosystem still settles to Ethereum. Owning ETH gives us the
  underlying claim plus optionality on every L2 that scales on top
  of it. We sit it as a 9% satellite, not core.
- **Solana** has the strongest consumer attach, but the validator
  economics and outage history keep it out of the core slot for now.
  6% satellite.

## What would change this view

We re-rate Base or Solana into the top slot only if **(a)** their
stablecoin float crosses 25% of the EVM total or **(b)** an
institutional ETF wrapper for either lists on a tier-1 venue.
